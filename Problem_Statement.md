Google Pay is a digital payment application used for sending and receiving money through UPI.

## Why Google Pay exists
- Many people do not want to carry cash
- Bank visits for small payments are inconvenient
- Users need a fast and simple way to transfer money

## Who uses Google Pay
- Students for daily expenses
- Working professionals
- Small shop owners

From my personal use, Google Pay is mainly helpful for quick payments such as splitting bills, paying friends, or small shop payments.
